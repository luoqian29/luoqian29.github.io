Overview

游戏概述
  • Apocalypse 2059 Welcome to the savage world of 2059, where survival is a daily struggle and civilization is nothing but a distant memory. The World That Was In 2030, an event known as “The Cataclysm” forever changed the face of Earth. Nuclear weapons claimed lives in an instant, plunging the world into a devastating nuclear winter that lasted over a decade. Civilization, law, and morality crumbled into dust. The surface became an inferno, forcing what little plant life remained to retreat underground. For the few who survived, existence became a constant battle against extinction. Twenty years have passed. The pre-war generation grows old, while a new generation rises, knowing nothing of the world that came before.
Spoiler
  • Your Story Begins The story takes place in the year 2059. You, the protagonist, have been living in solitude in a shelter since your birth. These peaceful days are changed by the appearance of a mysterious woman. At her urgent request, you’ll embark on a perilous journey across the savage lands of what was once civilization. Unforgettable Characters Your path will cross with unique survivors, each shaped by the merciless world they inhabit: A reckless and confident villain with her own agenda A young, sensitive tribal leader struggling to protect her people A ruthless warlord carving out her empire silver-tongued treasure hunter seeking fortune in the ruins
