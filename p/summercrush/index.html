Changelog

更新日志
  • The eighth and final episode of Summer Crush is now available. New scenes, animations, achievements, and music await you. Well, business as usual!
  • I realize I’ve been dragging my feet. In May 2025, when I sat down to develop the final episode, I thought I’d have it all finished in time for the game’s third anniversary, November 25th. And now, on November 24th, I’m looking at the game’s progress and realizing there’s still a ton of work to do: the animations aren’t ready, some renders aren’t finished, the music is missing, and there’s no sign of any translations into other languages. This post is partly for myself – if you’re the only one responsible for everything, don’t set deadlines! You’ll never make it…
  • Nevertheless, I apologize. The game was supposed to be finished sooner, but I couldn’t do it before. But now it’s done!
  • I hope you enjoy it. Thank you for devoting your precious time to my little game.
