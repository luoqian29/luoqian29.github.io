Changelog

更新日志
  • We’ve just fixed an issue that was affecting access to saves made before the last hotfix. The good news is: your pre-update saves SHOULD now work again! If we didn’t f*ck something up in the process, that is.
  • However, we also want to be honest with you — this fix may have caused some saves made after April 10th (in specific scenes) to no longer function correctly. We’re really sorry about that.
  • Over the past few days, we’ve been working hard to understand and solve this issue. Unfortunately, it was something out of our control — it’s a rare problem that can occur with the engine we use to build the game. This was our first time encountering it too.

  • Also, good news: we might have fixed the issue where the walkthrough wasn’t launching properly. If you had trouble with that before, please give it another try after this update and let us know if it works for you!
  • We’re really sorry for the inconvenience, and we hope those of you who started new saves recently will understand the situation.
