Overview

游戏概述
  • Shattered Minds is an open-world game, in which you take control over the life of a college student.
  • Your normal life is thrown out of whack after you realize you have the power to control the minds of others.
  • You will use this power to make all your dreams, desires and wishes come true!
  • A world with countless quests, figures, plots and locations are waiting to be discovered and controlled by you!
  • Shattered Minds includes plenty of animations, a licensed soundtrack and atmospheric sound effects!
  • Be ready to experience a game full of sexy adventures!
  • All paired with a thrilling, mysterious yet humorous plot.

Changelog

更新日志
  • Cursory Investigation (Claire – Quest 1)
  • A brief text message* calls you to Spring Hill Police Department. There you meet Claire again. From that point on you sense eyes on you wherever you go, and the shadow that trails you feels like the first clue that something larger is in motion…
  • (* the message triggers the next morning, once Scarlett is part of your harem)
  • Cat and Mouse (Claire – Quest 2) Armed with what you know about Claire’s motives, you decide to turn the tables. You slip deeper into her life until you are part of it, as you begin to undermine and manipulate the close bond between her and her father.
  • Case Closed (Claire – Quest 3)
  • The confrontation between Claire and the MC comes to a head. The MC was too sure of himself and underestimated how sharp a budding detective Claire really is. His overconfidence proves costly.
  • New harem member: Claire: Claire will become a regular members of your harem, with new scenes available for her at your home and at the police station. She can get pregnant and will be able to move into your house.
  • New Smartphone Wallpaper: Claire
  • New side character: Violet: Once you own the gym, she can be encountered in the gym locker room. She takes the new gym rules with enthusiasm… there’s just one problem, her boyfriend is also there. But that’s no challenge you can’t handle.
  • Quality of Life improvement: The Harem Tab now also shows if a harem member is currently set to earn money or XP.
  • Rebalancing: Your harem’s income has been greatly improved to reduce grind. Now, every member can be set to earn you $150/day or 30XP/day (up from $45/day or 15XP/day).
  • Engine Update: The game now runs on Ren’Py 8.4.1. This update improves loading times and resolves issues for operating systems that use non-Latin character sets (such as Russian, Chinese, etc.).
  • Translations: All translations have been updated to V0.21 level bug fixes, rebalancing and spelling corrections
